Open the project File
From left to right

- Draw Mode(Left)
  - click on the cells to draw color
  - click random to generate random maps
  - click redraw for more maps
  - click play to play the map
  - click save to save the map
  - from levels, click level 4 to load saved maps

- Levels Mode(Middle)
  - Level 1, 2, 3 provided
  - level 4 - 9 are for users to save their own maps

- Maze Mode(Right)
  - Press "1" for DFS generator
  - Press "2" for Prim generator
  - Press "3" to exit
